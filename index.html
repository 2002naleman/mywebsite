FAQ

When will you be graduating from SDSU?

I will be graduating from SDSU in Spring 2024, I plan to receive my Bachelors in Multimedia.

What do you have planned for after graduation?

Currently, I dont have a clear path after graduation. I hope to keep my part-time job and search for options that fall under my major.

What was your favorite class you took at SDSU?

Over the course of the two years I've spent at SDSU after transferring, I found ceramics to be one of my favorite classes. I've been having fun being hands one with the clay and creating various vessels and sculptures that I've taken quite a bit of time on and am proud to display and give to others.

What work are you most proud producing at SDSU?

The work I'm most proud in producing would have to be the sets of cups I made for my last project in beginning ceramics. That project was the most tedious as there are many steps in creating the vessel itself and choosing the look. I also found the statue inspired by ancient pottery to be another favorite as I had to remake the project due to it blowing up. The remaking of it was quite fast the second time and I found that I liked the look of it better than the first.
